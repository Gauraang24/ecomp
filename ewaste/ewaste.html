ROHS

We are fully aware of the responsibility to our environment as well as the world-wide limited resources.
Our products were developed for a long lifetime and low consumption of energy. In the sense of Green IT we make our contribution to the reduction of waste and saving of raw materials.
According to Directive 2002/95/EC of the European Community on the Restriction of the Use of Certain Hazardous Substances in Electrical and Electronic Equipment (RoHS), our products are free from lead, mercury, cadmium and other substances which are harmful to the environment respectively apply to the stringent limits. Such we help to protect the environment and to reduce the pollution of the nature.
RoHS is a directive within the European Union that is of great importance to manufacturers and suppliers of equipment. The letters stand for Restriction of Hazardous Substances, and its aim is to reduce the hazardous substances that are encountered in everyday life and also enter the ecosystem.
The application of the RoHS, Restriction of Hazardous Substances, directive is not only applicable to equipment manufactred within the EU, it also applies to items imported. In addition to this, many other countries outside the EU have similar legislation.
